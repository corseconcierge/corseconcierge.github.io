Corse: Italian, plural feminine, race, short trip.

Concierge: French, a contraction of “comte des cierges” – “count of candles”; a servant responsible for maintaining the lighting and cleanliness of medieval palaces.

Corse Concierge

Keeping your business on track

What we can do for you

Corse Concierge provides the following services to those within the motorsport industry, or those seeking to work with the sport:

  • Business direction, development and administration
  • Commercial and business strategy
  • Project management
  • Event organisation

This isn’t to say we can’t do other things – we love a challenge – but these are our strengths. We believe that people should do what they are best at, and enjoy doing, and that’s where we think we come in.

If you love nothing more than planning a pit stop strategy, but the thought of planning a business strategy leaves you cold, then let us take care of that.

How we work

Corse Concierge work with their clients in whatever way suits them best.

  • Project based
  • Hourly rate, ad-hoc
  • Daily rate, fixed term contract
  • On site
  • On event
  • Remote assistance

We like to get to know you and your business first. Preferably over a good cup of tea. You might have a specific event or project in mind. You might not know where to start. We don’t judge either way.

Who we are

Ella Barrington – Managing Director

A reformed engineer, Ella is an award-winning business and operations director for motorsport.

She has worked with and for teams and tier one suppliers in everything from karting to international sportscars, junior formula to historic racing.

Martyn Hoyer – Digital Director

Martyn is in charge of all things online. Through working for small agencies to multinational technology companies, Martyn has a passion for the web.

Combined with his knowledge of professional motorsport, he understands what works and what doesn’t across multiple platforms.

What we aspire to be

Secretly we’d like to be astronauts floating out in the great unknown, but for now we’ll settle for being trusted partners for forward-thinking companies.

We want to be the company you recommend without hesitation; the extra-set of eyes (and hands) for that project that seems a bit overwhelming; the people celebrating with you at the foot of the podium.
